# 9. GENERAL PHOTOGRAPHY GUIDELINES
*For all images submitted*

- Use **real photos** from your drives and programs — not stock photography
- Photos should be **warm, natural, and hopeful** in tone — not sad or exploitative
- Always get **written consent** before using photos of children or community members
- Minimum resolution: **150 DPI** for web use
- Preferred format: **JPG or PNG**
- Do **not** submit blurry, dark, or heavily filtered photos
- For child photos: faces can be shown with consent, or use back-of-head / activity shots if preferred
